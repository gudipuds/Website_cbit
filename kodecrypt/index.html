  • RULES AND REGULATIONS:

    1st Round Rules:
    1. Participant should be a Student of an Engineering College (B.E/B.TECH/M.C.A)
    2. Any kind of plagiarism will not be tolerated and will lead to the disqualification of the Candidate.
    3. Duration of 1st Round online exam will not be extended except problem is from our server side.
    2nd and 3rd Round Rules:
    1. Contestants should bring id card provided by kodecrypt team
    2. They will be provided with pen and rough paper.
    3. Communication between contestants is not permitted during the contest.
    4. Documentation for STL and Java will be provided.
    5. During the contest, contestants can make clarification request on problem statements.
    7. Different problems may be solved using different Programming Languages.
    8. Contestants are not allowed to use other applications in the system other than the provided environment.
    9. Any illegal access to the network/internet will not be tolerated and will lead to immediate disqualification of the contestant.
    10.judges decision is final and abiding.
    11. Judges may change selection procedure without any prior intimation to the participants.
    Selection procedure:

    1. A minimum of 1 question must be answered in order to qualify for the next round of the competition.
    2. Top 30 contestants are selected to the 3rd round who solve at least one problem.
    3. If two contestants solve the same number of problems, then the contestant who is the earliest in providing the solution will be considered for selection.
    4. If all of the above criterions are matched, then time and space complexity of the problem will be considered for selecting the contestant.

  • 1. What should I do after coming to the campus?

    Outstation participants who have registered for accommodation are requested to report at the registration desk where the required formalities involving checking of confirmation mail, your college ID card, allotment of rooms and mess registration will be finished. All the participants who are accommodated at CBIT are supposed to collect their HEADSTART ID Cards from the Hospitality team.


    2. What should I bring with me?

    a. It is mandatory for all participants carry your college IDs .
    b. If your accommodation is confirmed, you are required to bring a hard copy of the confirmation form which will be mailed to you.
