  • About Headstart'12

    “The computer revolution is a revolution in the way we think and in the way we express what we think.”

    - H. Abelson and G. Sussman

    Day after day, the world sings the same old song of technology-about how it revolutionized our interpretations of every event in the universe, whether it is visible to us or not. This song has now become an anthem as we march towards the inevitable. The year 2003 faced the inception of an effort to catch up with the future, the commencement of 'Headstart' at the Computer Science & Engineering department of CBIT, Hyderabad.

    This year we take up the mission of finding more solutions to the everyday challenges posed by the IT world around us.

    We come back with renewed energy, fresh spirits and charged minds and we are ready to break boundaries and enter new Frontiers! The department of Computer Science & Engineering proudly announces HEADSTART '12, a student's technical fest on 17th and 18th February 2012. Ask us who our role model is, we say Dennis Ritchie. With programming in our blood and printf statements rolling out of our tongues, we say it loud and we say it proud! Our student run organization has gathered up extremely helpful sponsors to help us achieve what we have envisioned. We have lined up events for students of all colleges to show off their techie skills. Along with other events which display the creativeness of our computer science students.

    In addition to the formal events like paper, Software, Hardware presentation contests designed for attracting future employers we also have informal events including Technical Quiz, Extempore and Network Games and other literary events are planned. Eminent personalities from Academics,industry will also be a part of this event to share experiences with excited yet-to-graduate students. We invite one and all to come forth on this day to celebrate with us our rich programming history.
